About & Contact

About This Tool

PoE2Settings.com was created by a hardcore ARPG player who has spent thousands of hours in Wraeclast — dying to porcupines, ripping in hardcore, and obsessing over every single frame in juiced T16 maps.

After the Path of Exile 2 Early Access launch, I noticed that most "optimization guides" were either too shallow ("just turn down your settings") or buried in 40-minute YouTube videos. I wanted something interactive, precise, and instantly actionable — a tool where you select your hardware and get a tailored production_Config.ini without editing random forum posts.

Every setting in this generator is backed by real testing — running maps, measuring frame times, and tweaking parameters until the 1% lows stop hurting. This is a tool by a player, for players.

Why This Exists

Path of Exile 2 is an incredible game, but its engine demands careful tuning. The difference between a smooth 144 FPS experience and a stuttery mess often comes down to five or six config file values that aren't even exposed in the in-game options menu. This tool bridges that gap.
